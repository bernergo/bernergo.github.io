Bärner Go Meetup

Join us  

We're a group for Go Developers in and around Berne. We encourage the use of the Go Programming Language (Golang) and offer a regular platform for presentations, networking, and exchange.

  • To find the next time we're getting together check our meetup page.
  • For past meetups, talks, or if you would like to give a talk, please check out our github page.
  • To contact us, give feedback or just hang out, check our slack channel.
